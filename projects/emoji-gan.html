Emoji
Generation GAN

A Generative Adversarial Network built in PyTorch that learns to generate novel emoji images from an open-source dataset. The project covered the full lifecycle — data pipeline, generator/discriminator training loop, and quality evaluation through sampled generations across epochs.

  • Two networks trained adversarially — a generator that synthesizes emoji from noise, and a discriminator that learns to tell real from generated.
  • Built the full training pipeline in PyTorch: data loading, training loop, checkpointing, and sampling.
  • Evaluated output quality by sampling generations across training epochs to track how realism improves over time.
