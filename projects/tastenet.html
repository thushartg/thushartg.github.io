TasteNet
Taste-Matched Restaurant Reviews

A Buffalo-based MVP that matches restaurant reviews to your palate instead of a generic star average. New users take a five-dimension taste quiz — spice, sweetness, saltiness, adventurousness, authenticity — and TasteNet scores that profile against reviewers with cosine similarity, surfacing the reviews (and restaurants) from people who actually taste the way you do.

Features

  • Onboarding quiz captures a taste profile across five slider dimensions instead of a single rating.
  • Cosine similarity matches a reader's profile against individual reviewers, not just the restaurant average.
  • Weighted rating system re-ranks reviews by how closely a reviewer's palate matches yours.
  • Restaurant listings surface a personalized taste-match score per user.
  • Auth and Postgres data layer handled by Supabase; hosted and deployed on Vercel.
